ABOUT HISP INDIA

HISP INDIA is a not for profit NGO specializing since more than a deacde in designing and implementing solutions in health informatics for the public health sector in indian states, and also recently in Bangladesh and Sri Lanka.

We are not a solely technology focused organization, and pride ourseleves for being multi-disciplinary and seeking to the knowledge domains of public health and informatics.
