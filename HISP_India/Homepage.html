ABOUT HISP INDIA

HISP INDIA is a not for profit NGO specializing since more than a decade in designing and implementing solutions in health informatics for the public health sector in Indian states, and also Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, Bhutan And others.

We pride ourselves for being multi-disciplinary and seeking to the Knowledge domain of public health, informatics and implementation research.

Strong commitment to free and open source software based solution and standard based solutions.

Our flagship products include DHIS2 and openMRS. We involve these products based on principles of global public goods.

Active engagement in global, regional and national level networks.

We are part of the global, regional HISP network since 2000. We leverage upon this global expertise and also contribute to the development of collective knowledge.

Actively combining system development, research and education.

We work within an action research framework that combine research, education and system development. Aimed at solving practical problem relating to health information system strengthening.

Products

These are core platforms which we are developing solution for different clients


DHIS-2 Aggregate

We have developed various applications for aggregated data such as state data warehouse and integrated analytical dashboard, reports and core customization of DHIS2 features.

DHIS-2 Tracker

Responding to the current trend for patient and event based system. We have carried out different customization for diseases registry clinic base system and support of patient level care.

DHIS-2 Mobile

DHIS2 Mobile helps the extension of DHIS2 based solution to cater to the demands. For example: Case based surveillance and reporting and supporting community health worker.

openMRS - Hospital

To support integrated patient based core within hospital and clinical settings, we have customized the openMRS platform and also integrated it with DHIS2 to enable aggregate facility reporting.

iHRIS

We have customized iHRIS for human resources and integrated data from iHRIS and HMIS system to develop cross cutting indicators to gain insight into health workforce utilization and performance.

Services

Our core Services which will help you to find better solutions

Data Analytics

Using the DHIS2 we conduct monthly HMIS data Analysis on data quality and health status. These analytical reports, also available through the dashboard which are sent monthly to the states who use it for internal review and planning purposes and further dissemination to the District.

Integration Services

We have developed various integration services using the DHIS2 API functionality. Some of these services include integration with third party tools like ODK, Commcare and also with DHIS2 mobile data. It will make a better use of information across various systems.

DHIS2 Academies

Since 2012, we have been organizing DHIS2 Academy on various topics in collaboration with University of Oslo for DHIS2 users in the asia region. Topics covered till date Have included various fundamentals of DHIS2 around different use cases.

In-service Training

We have been actively undertaking In-service training from Indian states, since nearly a decade between 2008 and 2012. In partnership with NHSRC. We practiced in Delhi 1,000 man days of training across nearly 20 states. we are routinely conducting In-service training for states on use of DHIS2 and use of information for monitoring and evaluation.

Requirement and Evaluation Studies
