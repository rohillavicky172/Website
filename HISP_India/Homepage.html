ABOUT HISP INDIA

HISP INDIA is a not for profit NGO specializing since more than a deacde in designing and implementing solutions in health informatics for the public health sector in Indian states, and also Sri lanka, Bangladesh, Bhutan And Others.

We pride ourselves for being mutli-disciplinary and seeking to the Knowledge domain of public health, informatics and implementation research.

Strong commitment to free and open source software based solution and standard based solutions.

Our Flagship products include DHIS2 and openMRS. we involve these products based on Principles of global public goods.

Active engagement in Gloabl, Regional and National level Networks.

We are part of the Gloabl, Regional HISP Network since 2000. We levrage upon this global expertise and also contribute to the development of collective Knowledge.

Actively combining System development, research and Education.

We work within an action research framework that combine research, Education and system development. Aimed at solving practical problem relating to health information system Strengthening.

Our Products

Our Core platforms which we are developing solution for different clients includes


DHIS-2 Aggregate

We have developed various applications for aggregated data such as state data warehouse and integrated analytical dashboard , reports and core customization of DHIS2 features.

DHIS-2 Tracker

Responding to the current trend for patient and event based system, we have carried out different customization for diseases registery clinic base system and support of patient level care.

DHIS-2 Mobile

DHIS2 Mobile help the extension of DHIS2 based solution to catter to the demands for example of case based survillance and reporting and supporting community health worker.

openMRS - Hospital

To Support integrated patient based core within hospital and clinical settings, we have customized the openMRS platform and also integrated it with DHIS2 to enable aggergate facility reporting.

iHRIS

We have customized iHRIS for human resources and integrated data from iHRIS & HMIS system to develop cross cutting indicators to gain insites into health workforce utilization and Performance.

Data Analytics

Using the DHIS2 we conduct monthly HMIS data Analysis on data quality and health status. These analytical reports, also avilable through the dashboard are sent monthly to the states who use it for internal review and Planning purposes and further dissemination to the District.
