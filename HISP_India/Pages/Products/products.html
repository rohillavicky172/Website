Our Products

Hisp India provides services around a suite of health informatics products for the public health sector. All these products are based on free and open source platforms to provide the users with the freedom of having full control of their applications without the risk of vendor lock-ins to high-cost proprietary licenses.

DHIS2

Enabling data-warehousing functionalities. An essential step in strengthning HISs will be to link information production to use.

To support each of the products mentioned above-DHIS2, DHIS Tracker, DHIS Mobile, DHIS Hospital, Ihris- HISP India offers a renge of services including systems study and requirement analysis, customization and adaptation, new functionality development, capacity building, Handholding, troubleshooting, and the mangement of services.
