Our Services

We offer services aimed at improving the overall quality of the solutions, and encouraging the "use of the information for local action" which we believe is the cornerstone of an effective HIS.

Data Analytics

Using the DHIS2 we conduct monthly HMIS data Analysis on data quality and health status. These analytical reports, also avilable through the dashboard are sent monthly to the states who use it for internal review and Planning purposes and further dissemination to the District.

Integration Services

We have developed various integration services using the DHIS2 API functionality. Some of these services include integration with third party tools like ODK, Commcare and also with DHIS2 mobile data. It will make a better use of information accross various systems.

DHIS2 Academies

Since 2012, We have been Organizing DHIS2 Academy on various topics in Collaboration with University of Oslo for DHIS2 users in the Asia Region. Topics covered till date have included fundamentals of DHIS2, DHIS2-tracker, use of information and DHIS2 for disease survillance.

InService Training

We have been actively undertaking InService Trainingfrom Indian states, since nearly a deacde between 2008 and 2012. In partnership with NHSRC. We particed in Delhi 1000 man days of Training accross nearly 20 states. we are routinely conducting inservice Training for states on use of DHIS2 and use of information for Monitoring and Evaluation.

Requirement and Evaluation Studies

All our projects have a strong component of Requirement analysis where we understand issues relating to data flow meta-data and the indicators require. The aim is through this analysis conducted incollaboration with clients to not only automate existing processes but to also rationalize and integrate them. So as to strengthen information use and to reduce data collection burdens.
