Technical Support Engineer L1 -L2

Be Our Consultant

We welcome proposals from qualified consultants to provide services on specific aspects related to health information systems, data analytics, server management services, and organizational development

Responsibilities:
• Provide technical support for both hardware and software issues to our users encounter
• Manage the configuration and operation of client-based computer operating systems
• Monitor the system daily and respond immediately to security or usability concerns
• Create and verify backups of data
• Respond to and resolve help desk requests
• Upgrade systems and processes as required for enhanced functionality and security issue resolution
• Administrate infrastructure, including firewalls, databases, malware protection software and other processes
• Review application logs

Skills Required
• 2+ years’ experience in system administration
• Associate or higher degree in a related field
• Extensive experience with kvm
• Good Linux Skill is must
• Good Knowledge of LAMP server and Tomcat Server
• Advanced knowledge of system vulnerabilities and security issues
• Basic understanding of insurance goals and practices
• Ability to respond to help desk requests after hours on a limited basis
