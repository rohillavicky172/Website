Our Projects

Hisp India provides services around a suite of health informatics products for the public health sector. All these products are based on free and open source platforms to provide the users with the freedom of having full control of their applications without the risk of vendor lock-ins to high-cost proprietary licenses.

Projects

A common theme running through all these projects is the focus on free and open source software all for the purpose of supporting public Health systemsin India, and in the recent years also abroad. We provide a brief outline of some of these projects starting from the most recent(including those in the stage of finalization) and going down to when we started in 1999. The projects are described over two broad categories of National and Global.
